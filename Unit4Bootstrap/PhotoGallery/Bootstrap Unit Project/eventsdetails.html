Event Details

July 15-August 6 2024

The Lake Front fair begins July 15 with a extravigent Firework show at night. The Fair will end on August 6 conculding with a parade, concert, and polar plunge. On July 24, the Annual Art Show will take place inside the Fair and Along the shoreline and Boardwalk. Yoga on the Beach will take place every Saturday at 10am. Fair Rides will be closed on the 26th for matinence.

The Fair is filled with many activites! There are Teacup rides for the little ones, carnival games for the Family, Rollercoasters for the Teens, Resturants and bars for the adults, Bingo for the seniors and Many More. Lake Front Fair has a thing for Everyone!

Speakers:
  • Mayor Cooper
  • The Mayor of Lake Front County, George Cooper, who has been in office since 2015. A former Police Officer who has served this town for 25 years prior to being elected.
  • David Jean
  • The owner of Don's Planners, David Jean, who planned this years event. The son of Don Jean who founded the event planning Buisness David runs today.
