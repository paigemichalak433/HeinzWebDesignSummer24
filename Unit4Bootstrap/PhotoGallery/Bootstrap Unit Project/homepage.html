Lake Front Fair


Welcome!

Welcome to the Offical website of the Lake Front Fair!

The Lake Front Fair is a annual Fair full of fun, food, and laughter. Located off the shore of Lake Michigan you can experience the joyfull and booming crowds and endless fun in the fair and resturants and get to experience the calming tranquility of Lake Michigan. It is Fair not to miss. The fun never ends!

Don's Planners

Huge shoutout to Don's Planners Event Planning for planning and hosting the fair each and every year. Don's Planners is a multigenerational planning company who never siezes to impress the community with there well thought out ideas. If you have a party to plan, Don is your man!
