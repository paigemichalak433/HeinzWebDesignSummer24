Privacy Concerns and Solutions

Cookies and Their Role: Cookies are saved information about you on a certain website. They are saved for you the next time you visit that site, like your password or username.

Privacy Concerns with Cookies and Third-Party Cookies: Cookies also save detailed information about you, like your intrests, to make targeted content toward you. Third-Party Cookies are cookies that come from sites you are not on and can track you from site to gather information which could be sold to other companies.

How diffrent browsers address privacy concerns; Private Browsing or Ad Tracking Prevention?: Some Browsers allow you to block Third-Party Cookies and offer private browsing settings. Private Browsers does not hide your information from other websites, just to other people on the same device.

How a Browser Views a Webpage

How does a Browser fetch and display data from webpages: A browser uses a rendering machine to translate the HTML code into text and images for the viewer to see.

Significance of HTML in creating structures of Webpages: The data for a website is written in HTML and Web Browsers read this code to create the text, images, audio, and other stuff we see and hear on websites.

Use of Hyperlinks and URLs in navagating between pages: Hyperlinks allow users to follow a path to other websites. A URL is a websites Web address. Every Webpage, image, and even video have one!
