All about them

Squirrels:

An agile tree-dwelling rodent with a bushy tail, typically feeding on nuts and seeds

Types of Squirrels:

There are around 268 diffrent species of Rodents and 122 of them are what most people consider a squirrel. The North American Grey Squirrel lives in Urban areas and is commonly seen in out day to day. The Red Squirrel lives in mostly Northern Europe. In certain villages in tropical regions squirrels are kept as pets, in others they are used as food.
