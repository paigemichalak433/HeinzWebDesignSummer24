Our Mission:Lake Front Fair centered around the fun and joy of the community, focuses on ensuring happiness, safety and enjoyment to the whole community, no matter the age!

Lake Front Fair's vision is to build the foundation to a happy life for all by building community, happiness, and comfort.

Goals:

Lake Front Fair Plans to expand the fair ground down the coast line and renovating the playgrounds, outdoor seating, restrooms, and parking lots; by 2025.

Don's Planners

Don's Planners, A multigenerational family run planning buisness, planned and executed this Fair for 20 years. Don, the founder, has been a major contributor to this town and his core motto is, "Having Fun is the foundation to a happy life". Once Don heard of the city proposing the idea of a Fair, Don bought in very quickly. Him and his Son, David, brainstormed, planned, and executed Their ideas for this Fair and each year they get more Bold. Sadly, Don passed on his buisness, name, and legacy to his son last year, when passed. In Honor of Don, David make sure to keep the fair going.
